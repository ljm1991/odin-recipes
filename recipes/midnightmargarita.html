Midnight Margaritas

Description

This famously sweet, sour, and salty drink will forever be an all-time favorite. For only having four ingredients, this drink packs quite a punch and is sure to soothe any and all troubles.

Ingredients

  • 2 oz blanco tequila
  • Juice of 1 Whole Lime
  • 1/2 oz of triple sec
  • Salt for rim (optional)
  • Lime wedge, for garnish

Steps

  1. Salt rim of a highball or footed glass
  2. Fill cocktail shaker with ice
  3. Add tequila, lime juice, and triple sec
  4. Shake and pour into highball or footed glass
  5. Garnish with wedge of lime

Note, while this concoction can be enjoyed at any hour, it is most enjoyable and likely most effective at midnight.
